Kodaikanal

It referred to as "Princess of Hills".It is a lakeside resort town witha beautiful climate, mist-covered manicured cliffs, and waterfalls that come together to create the ideal setting for a perfect getaway.

Time to visit - Kodaikanal in Summer (April to june)

Kodaikanal in Monsoon (July to September)

Kodaikanal in Winter (December to February)

Ooty

It's called "blue mountains", so named due to the kuruji flower which used to give slopes a bluish tinge. Ooty is one of the most beautiful hill stations in india, nestled in the Niligiri district, and is a popular tourist destination.

Time to visit - Ooty in Summer (March to June)

Ooty in Winter (October to March)

Ooty in Monsoon (July to September)

Goa

It is a state in western india with coastlines streching along the Arabian Sea.Goa attracts more foriegners and domestic tourists. It popular for beaches, along side major diversity we can found in here.

Time to visit - Goa in Off Season (April to June)

Goa in Moderate Season (July to October)

Goa in Peak Season (November to March)

Kerala

It is popular destination both domestic and foriegn tourists. Kerala is well known for its beaches, backwaters in alappuzha and kollam, mountain ranges and wild life sanctuaries.

Time to Visit - Kerala in Summer (September to March)

Kerala in Winter (April to June)

Kerala in Monsoon(July to August)
